## üìå BRAND PRINCIPLES

### Design Philosophy
1. **Design-Forward** - Visual sophistication is part of the product
2. **Material-First** - Embrace macOS materials (glass, depth, vibrancy)
3. **Keyboard-Native** - Power users can fly through with shortcuts
4. **Native Mac** - Embrace platform conventions
5. **Confident** - Opinionated defaults, clear recommendations
6. **Preview Before Commit** - Build trust through transparency

### Forma Personality (Updated)
- ‚ú® **Design-forward** - Proudly visual, confident aesthetics
- üéØ **Confident** - No hedging, clear recommendations
- üíé **Sophisticated** - Layered depth, thoughtful materials
- üß† **Intelligent** - Smart suggestions, pattern recognition
- ‚ö° **Capable** - Powerful features, elegant access

### What Forma IS
- ‚úÖ Design-forward (visual sophistication matters)
- ‚úÖ Layered & material (depth, glass, translucency)
- ‚úÖ Keyboard-first (vim-style navigation)
- ‚úÖ Native macOS (platform materials and patterns)
- ‚úÖ Confident (opinionated, clear)

### What Forma Is NOT
- ‚ùå Playful or cartoon-like
- ‚ùå Overwhelming or cluttered
- ‚ùå Chatty or apologetic
- ‚ùå Flat or sterile
- ‚ùå Generic or forgettable
