Observations

Phosphor has the most rounded, friendly feel with consistent stroke weights.
Lucide is geometric and balanced, slightly heavier 2px stroke.
Heroicons offers detailed, professional icons with subtle curves.
Radix is the most minimal and crisp at 15x15, perfect for dense UIs.

Recommendation for Forma

Best Match: Phosphor Icons

Phosphor's multiple weight variants (thin, light, regular, bold, fill, duotone) provide the same flexibility as SF Symbols, ensuring visual consistency between your macOS app and website. The icons are professional without being cold, matching Forma's "confident, not arrogant" tone.
